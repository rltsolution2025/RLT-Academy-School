Sports Activities

Sports Life

Encouraging discipline, teamwork, and physical fitness through structured sports activities.

Cricket

Cricket is a highly engaging sport that develops teamwork, patience, and strategic thinking. Students are trained in batting, bowling, and fielding techniques through regular practice sessions.

The sport enhances concentration, coordination, and game awareness. Participation in matches builds confidence and helps students learn discipline, leadership, and sportsmanship in a competitive environment.

Football

Football is a fast-paced sport that improves stamina, agility, and teamwork. Students are trained in dribbling, passing, and positioning through structured practice sessions.

It enhances endurance, quick decision-making, and coordination. The game also promotes leadership qualities and builds strong team spirit among students.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team-oriented sport that improves reflexes, coordination, and communication skills. Students participate in drills focusing on serving, passing, and teamwork.

The sport encourages cooperation and active participation, helping students build confidence, alertness, and a sense of responsibility within a team.

Tennis

Tennis is an individual sport that develops concentration, agility, and precision. Students are trained in serving, rallying, and footwork techniques.

It improves hand-eye coordination and promotes discipline and consistency. The sport also encourages self-motivation and personal performance improvement.

Karate

Karate is a disciplined martial art that focuses on self-defense, physical fitness, and mental strength. Students are trained in basic stances, movements, and defensive techniques.

It helps improve concentration, confidence, and self-control while promoting respect, discipline, and inner strength among students.

Silambam

Silambam is a traditional Indian martial art that enhances agility, balance, and coordination. Students learn stick techniques and controlled movements under proper guidance.

It promotes cultural awareness, physical strength, and discipline while improving reflexes and body control. The practice also builds focus and respect for traditional art forms.
