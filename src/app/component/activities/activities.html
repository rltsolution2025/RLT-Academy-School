School Activities

Events & Celebrations

Discover a vibrant range of school events that inspire creativity, build confidence, and promote cultural and national values among students.

Sports Day Activities

Sports Day at RLT Academy is a vibrant and energetic event that celebrates physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. It provides students with an opportunity to showcase their talents, participate in competitive events, and experience the spirit of healthy competition.

The event is carefully organized with a variety of track and field activities, fun games, and team events that encourage participation from all students. It also helps in building confidence, discipline, and coordination while creating memorable experiences.

  • Track Events – Running races, relays, and sprints
  • Field Events – Long jump, shot put, and athletics
  • Team Games – Football, cricket, and group competitions
  • Fun Activities – Tug of war, obstacle races, and drills

Annual Day Celebration

Annual Day at RLT Academy is a grand celebration that showcases the talents, creativity, and achievements of our students. It is one of the most awaited events of the year, bringing together students, teachers, and parents in a joyful and vibrant atmosphere.

The event features a variety of cultural performances, stage presentations, and award ceremonies that highlight the academic and co-curricular excellence of students. It provides a platform for students to express their talents, build confidence, and develop stage presence.

  • Cultural Performances – Dance, music, and drama
  • Stage Presentations – Skits and thematic programs
  • Awards & Recognition – Academic and activity achievements
  • Special Events – Guest speeches and celebrations

Republic Day Celebration

Republic Day at RLT Academy is celebrated with great pride and patriotism, honoring the values and principles of our nation. The celebration instills a sense of responsibility, unity, and respect for the country among students.

The event includes ceremonial activities, cultural programs, and performances that reflect the rich heritage and diversity of India. Students actively participate in various activities, showcasing their talents while understanding the significance of this national occasion.

  • Flag Hoisting and National Anthem
  • Patriotic Performances – Dance, songs, and speeches
  • March Past and Drill Displays
  • Awareness Programs on National Values

Independence Day Celebration

Independence Day at RLT Academy is celebrated with great enthusiasm and patriotism, honoring the sacrifices of our freedom fighters and the spirit of our nation. The celebration inspires students to value unity, responsibility, and national pride.

The event includes a range of cultural programs and activities that reflect India’s rich heritage and traditions. Students actively participate in performances and presentations, gaining awareness about the importance of independence and national values.

  • Flag Hoisting and National Anthem
  • Patriotic Songs, Dance, and Speeches
  • Skits on Freedom Fighters and History
  • Awareness Programs on National Unity and Values
