About RLT Academy

India's First Bagless Education System

RLT Academy Matriculation Higher Secondary School is a forward-thinking institution focused on transforming traditional education. We introduce India's first bagless learning environment, reducing student burden while enhancing conceptual understanding.

Our cloud-based learning system enables students to access study materials, assignments, and digital classrooms anytime, anywhere. We blend academic excellence with innovation, ensuring holistic development and future-ready skills.
